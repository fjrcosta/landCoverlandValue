Land Cover Classification Maps

Deep learning-based urban land cover classification using freely avaliable high resolution satellite imagery from Google Earth.

CLIP RSICD

Contrastive Language-Image Pre-Training (CLIP) model with Vision Transformer (ViT-B/32) backbone, fine-tuned on the RSICD dataset, used as a feature extractor.

Specifications:
• Spatial Resolution: 109×109m
• Year: 2024
View CLIP Classification Map

DINOv2 ViTL/14

Self-supervised vision transformer (DINOv2) with Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) for efficient fine-tuning.

Specifications:
• Spatial Resolution: 109×109m
• Year: 2024
